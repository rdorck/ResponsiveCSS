2012-13 Distinguished Speaker Series

The Department of Computer and Information Sciences proudly announces a special distinguished speaker series featuring prominent women in computer science.

Topics covered include programming for software defined networks, cloud computing and privacy and security. An informal question and answer session with the distinguished speaker will follow each lecture.

The series is co-sponsored by the department and the University of Delaware NSF-ADVANCE and PDI STEM initiatives. It is organized in part by the CISters (Women in Computer and Information Sciences) group and the WIE (Women in Engineering) program.

The speaker series is free and open to the public. For event information, call 302-831-2712.
