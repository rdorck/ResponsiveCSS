Research

Laboratories

The Computer and Information Sciences Department has established research laboratories for each of the areas listed below. These laboratories include specialized equipment, graduate student offices, and workspace for undergraduate researchers. The research area laboratories facilitate group effort by faculty, graduate students, and undergraduates, and the close proximity of the group members enhances the mentoring relationship between senior and junior researchers.

Brief summary of CIS laboratories:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Bioinformatics
  • Computer Graphics
  • Computer Networks & Distributed Systems
  • Dynamic Vision
  • High Performance Computing
  • Natural Language Processing & Information Retrieval
  • Software Engineering
  • Theory of Computation
